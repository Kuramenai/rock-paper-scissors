How to play ?
If you have played rock paper scissors before, you won't have any problem playing this simple web-based version. The only difference here is that your opponent is the computer.
But if this is your first time playing, don't worry too much the rules are quite simple. You just need to select an item from the three ones proposed, that is either rock, paper(money) or scissors. When you click on the item of your choice, the computer will also randomly select one.

The outcome is determined by the following rules:
  • Rock beats Scissors
  • But Rock is weak against Paper
  • Paper itself is weak against Scissors
 As you see the rules are quite simple.
But always remember the one who first reaches 5 wins is the winner.
